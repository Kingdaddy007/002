## The Core Goal & Brand Purpose (Reminder)
The goal is to solve the "Render vs. Reality" perception gap. The user must interact with a physical material (`[Stone] [Steel] [Wood]`) before viewing the portfolio.
- **The Canvas:** Dark charcoal (`#121212`), absolute silence.
- **The Anchor:** Warm champagne (`#D4C5B3`) SVG vector path tracing the XBD monogram.
- **The Interaction:** Clinical HUD prompting `[SELECT MATERIAL] ➔ [01/STONE] [02/STEEL] [03/WOOD]`.
- **The Shutter Split:** Clicking a material splits the background apart to reveal the site.
