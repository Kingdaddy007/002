# Visual Thesis: XBD Collective
**Status:** Design Contract & Sensory Anchor
**Git Branch:** `ai-2-vision`
**Relative Workspace Directory:** `../002-other-ai`

---

## 1. The Core Rule
> **"The website behaves as a physical volume: a sequence of tactile stone-like spaces where the user's scroll unmasks the work and proves the transition from raw structure to finished design."**

---

## 2. Spatial Archetype
*   **Primary Archetype:** *Spatial Systems Credibility* (selling process, execution, and architectural rigor).
*   **Secondary Archetype:** *Gallery Procession* (understated, full-bleed images, quiet metadata text labels, room-by-room pacing).

---

## 3. Sensory Impression (The First 5 Seconds)
*   **Chosen Sensation:** *Hush & Awe.* A sudden transition from the noisy, busy web to a completely silent, clean, matte-dark canvas, followed by the slow, deliberate unmasking of Solar House at night.
*   **Signature Material:** *Limestone block* (conveyed through warm beige/cream background `#F5F2EB` and sharp, heavy vertical and horizontal slide wipes).
*   **Signature Threshold:** *The Typographic Curtain* (letters that split apart to reveal the workspace behind them).
