# Room Sequence (Homepage Section Map): XBD Collective
**Status:** Page Structure & Content Map
**Git Branch:** `ai-2-vision`
**Relative Workspace Directory:** `../002-other-ai`

This file outlines the exact sections we need to create for the homepage, explaining the purpose, visual content, and copywriting hooks for each step down to the footer.

---

## The Section Map: Overview
Our homepage is structured as 7 contiguous sections (or "rooms"):

1.  **Section 1: The Preloader** (Foyer/Threshold)
2.  **Section 2: The Typographic Curtain** (Hero/First Fold)
3.  **Section 3: The Intimacy Anchor** (Private Residential Room)
4.  **Section 4: The Scale Transition** (Commercial Developer Room)
5.  **Section 5: The Execution Proof** (Construction/Transformation Room)
6.  **Section 6: The Catalog Grid** (Range/Showcase Room)
7.  **Section 7: The Concierge Exit** (Inquiry/Footer)

---

## Detailed Section Specifications

### Section 1: The Preloader
*   **Visual:** Solid dark Charcoal background (`#121212`). Absolute silence. 
*   **Purpose:** Resets the visitor's focus. Restores space.
*   **Content/Copy:** A single thin Warm Champagne (`#D4C5B3`) vector path drawing the XBD infinity logo in the center of the viewport, which then fades out.

### Section 2: The Typographic Curtain (Hero)
*   **Visual:** The preloader fades, leaving the dark screen. Massive display typography appears in the center.
*   **Purpose:** Establishes philosophical and typographic authority before showing any images.
*   **Primary Copy:** `"THE REALITY OF THE EXTRAORDINARY."` (Display Serif)
*   **Secondary/Utility Copy:** `"X B D   C O L L E C T I V E"` (Geometric Sans-serif, pinned to the top/margins).
*   **Motion:** As the user scrolls, the logotype letters split and slide off-screen to clear the canvas.

### Section 3: The Intimacy Anchor (Solar House)
*   **Visual:** A full-screen, high-resolution night photograph of **Solar House** (glowing pool, warm interiors, starry night sky).
*   **Purpose:** Establishes elite B2C residential design capability and creates immediate visual depth.
*   **Primary Copy:** `"SOLAR HOUSE"` (Serif, sliding up behind the building roofline).
*   **Utility/Specs Copy:** `"PRIVATE RESIDENCE | 18,000 SQ FT | DUBAI"` (Sans-serif, tracked, placed under the title).

### Section 4: The Scale Transition (Rixos Residences)
*   **Visual:** A solid Limestone container (`#F5F2EB`) slides vertically over the dark Solar House section. Inside is a massive, bright daytime shot of the **Rixos Residences** skyscraper.
*   **Purpose:** Proves B2B developer scale. Establishes the tension between private intimacy and commercial height.
*   **Primary Copy:** `"RIXOS RESIDENCES"` (Serif, sliding in from the left).
*   **Utility/Specs Copy:** `"DEVELOPER COMMISSION | 76 STORIES | DOWNTOWN DUBAI"` (Sans-serif, uppercase).

### Section 5: The Execution Proof (Drawing to Object)
*   **Visual:** The background remains Limestone. A massive typographic statement dominates the screen.
*   **Purpose:** Directly attacks the perception gap (AI renders vs. finished execution). Proves they build what they draw.
*   **Primary Copy:** `"FROM DRAWING TO OBJECT."`
*   **Supporting Copy:** `"We build what others only render. Real materials, executed to the structural millimeter."` (Small paragraph).
*   **Content:** The letters of the primary copy act as transparent window masks. Inside the letters, a video loop plays, showing architectural wireframes transitioning to raw concrete construction columns, and finally resolving into a finished, polished lobby.

### Section 6: The Catalog Grid (Range)
*   **Visual:** Warm Champagne background (`#D4C5B3`) with generous negative space. A 3-column asymmetric masonry grid.
*   **Purpose:** Displays their diverse project categories (Kata Restaurant, Opus Tower, Althuraya Island) in a single fluid gesture without using generic carousel sliders.
*   **Content/Copy:**
    *   *Column 1 (Left):* Kata Restaurant ➔ `"KATA | COMMERCIAL F&B | 6,500 SQ FT"`
    *   *Column 2 (Center):* Althuraya Island ➔ `"ALTHURAYA | LUXURY WATERFRONT VILLAS"`
    *   *Column 3 (Right):* Opus Commercial Lobby ➔ `"OPUS TOWER | HIGH-END OFFICE LOBBY"`
*   **Motion:** Opposing scroll parallax. Left and right columns scroll upwards at different rates, while the center column translates downwards.

### Section 7: The Concierge Exit (Footer)
*   **Visual:** A solid Charcoal (`#121212`) block slides up over the grid. The screen ends in full dark mode, mirroring the preloader.
*   **Purpose:** Selective, high-trust inquiry.
*   **Primary Copy:** `"A digital presence of equal weight."`
*   **Concierge Link/CTA:** `"COMMISSION THE INVISIBLE"` (Warm Champagne link that opens email client or simple overlay).
*   **Footer Copy:** 
    *   Left side: `"XBD COLLECTIVE | DUBAI & LONDON"`
    *   Right side: `"© 2026. ALL RIGHTS RESERVED."`
